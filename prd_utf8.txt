Service Operations & Resource Management Dashboard

1. Project Overview
The purpose of this project is to build an internal web-based operations dashboard for a service-oriented company currently managing operations through multiple Excel sheets.
The system will centralize employee deployment, project allocation, revenue tracking, utilization monitoring, and operational analytics into a single interactive dashboard application.
The platform will reduce manual calculations, improve operational visibility, and support business decision-making through automated metrics and reporting.
